Park-in-a-Box reservation system

This page contains a list available GarageBoxes. The following functions are available:

  1. You can check the GarageBox availability.
  2. You can consult details of the GarageBox.
  3. You can have a look at the GarageBox location (image).
  4. You can reserve the GarageBox of your choice.
  5. You can hover over the different areas to get instructions about it's purpose and/or possible actions.
